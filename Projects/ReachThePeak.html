Reach The Peak

Reach The Peak is a (singleplayer and/or) online multiplayer Co-op game where players must survive in an endless maze of dungeons. Collect skill books that grant you offensive, defensive, and utility skills to enhance your chances of survival. As you progress, you’ll need to grow stronger to venture deeper into the abyss and ultimately reach the peak of your abilities to escape the labyrinth of dungeons!
