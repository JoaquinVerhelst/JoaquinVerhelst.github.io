Binary Bandits

Infiltrate the evil Robot Corp as a stealthy Spy with one mission: destroy their supercomputer hidden in the back of the facility. Along the way, you'll face laser grids, security cameras, and alert office workers. Thankfully, the Hacker is there to help, hacking into systems to disable cameras, open doors, and create distractions so the Spy can move forward.

If the Spy sets off alarms, the Hacker has a limited chance to override them. But be careful—three failed attempts, and the alarm is fully triggered. If caught, the Spy will be thrown out by the robots, forcing them to start again.

What I Did

This is my first Unreal Engine project, where I fully embraced the C++ side of UE and kept the usage of blueprints to the minimum. Throughout the development, I gained a deeper understanding of how Unreal Engine operates. I built the Spy player controller from scratch, programmed everything related to the AI for the enemies, and created the hackable objects, including doors, cameras, and lasers.

Flora Frenzy

Flora Frenzy is an action-packed shooter adventure game in which you have to escape from a hostile alien planet. Fight your way through waves of plant-based enemies, use different combat abilities and smoothly manoever your way across the levels. It's time to kick some plant butt!

What I Did

When we first started developing this game, we were full of excitement and ambition, eager to pack in as many mechanics as possible. However, this project taught me a valuable lesson: sometimes you need to adapt, scrap, or modify ideas to stay on schedule or improve the overall gameplay experience.

In this game, I took on the task of developing the entire player controller, with a significant focus on making the slide mechanics feel smoother and more responsive. I also worked on the enemy spawning system, wave and arena systems, and contributed a little bit to enemy behavior, like handling stuns, health, and death mechanics. Additionally, I was involved in implementing visual effects, player guidance UI, and various other small but important details that helped enhance the final product.
